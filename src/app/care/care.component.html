CAUTION

Your acecraft garment is special. It needs the right care to last longer and age well.

Wash less.

Wash delicately.

The truth is, the more you wash a cotton garment, the faster it wears out. Wash your acecraft garments delicately by hand or in the most gentle machine cycle. It does not need to be in water for more than 15 minutes. Always wash whites with whites and darks with darks.

DELICATE WASH
WASH INSIDE OUT
NO TWISTING

Easy on the detergents.

Chemicals are what they are. While they clean a garment, they also take away the good stuff. Use minimal detergent and no bleach. Avoid putting detergent directly on the fabric that can leave spots. Dry cleaning is extreme, only your blazers will require it.

MILD DETERGENT
NO BLEACH
DRY CLEANING

Hand dry.

Those spinning and drying machines are super harsh. Hang or flat dry your acecraft product. Avoid exposure to harsh sunlight as that can cause the colour to fade.

HANG DRY
FLAT AIR DRY
NO TUMBLE DRY

Correct grooming.

Place the iron on full heat while the garment is still damp. Iron carefully without stretching. Use a lint roller on your blazers so that they continue to look new.

STEAM IRON
USE LINT ROLLER
Follow the guidelines, wash your product right and it will look great. If you still have any issues, let us know. We want you to love wearing your uniforms.
